What started as a small family workshop has grown into one of Uttarakhand's most trusted glass businesses.

Our Founding

The New Jain Glass Story

In 1998, our founder started New Jain Glass with a single glass-cutting machine, a rented workshop, and an unshakeable commitment to quality. Word spread quickly — contractors began calling, then architects, then hotels.

Today, the second generation has joined the business, bringing modern techniques while preserving the personal attention that made us who we are.

We still treat every order — whether a single table top or a full hotel glass partition project — with the same care as our very first job.
